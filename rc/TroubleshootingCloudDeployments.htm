Task 1 - Troubleshooting Deployment Concepts

A deployment in cloud computing refers to any service, application, or resource you have decided to create and deploy to an infrastructure.

Due to the nature of working infrastructures and organizational IT, from time-to-time issues will arise that need to be resolved in a timely, preventive, and investigative manner to ensure that these issues are limited in future deployments.

It's key to consider that in real-world scenarios, it is normally a combination of multiple of the above that could cause any potential issue, and everything should be investigated together.

For example, an application in an infrastructure that powers a series of compute resources could fail due to networking issues on an ISP's end. Because of this, it's important to follow proper Disaster Recovery (DR) and Incident Response (IR) procedures to analyze and remediate the root cause.

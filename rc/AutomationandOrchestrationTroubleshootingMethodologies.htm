Exercise 1 - Troubleshooting Automation and Orchestration in the Cloud

Automation refers to automating singular processes to increase workflow and reduce the amount of manual time spent performing certain tasks, and Orchestration refers to managing several automated processes. Because of the automatic nature of performing these tasks however, several issues can arise that may need to be troubleshooted in a live, real-world environment.

In this exercise, we will explore potential issues with automation and orchestration concepts and troubleshooting methods.

Task 1 - Troubleshooting Automation and Orchestration Concepts

Automation and orchestration can be configured for different purposes, such as implementing Infrastructure as Code, Continuous Integration and Deployment (CI/CD), Containerization and more. Due to this, remediating potential issues depends on the root cause of an issue.

It’s important to consider that automation is not a “be all end all” solution in real-world environments and cannot be applied as a band-aid fix with an already poorly functioning workflow.
